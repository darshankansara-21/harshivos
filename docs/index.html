HARSHIVOS

A calm, magical sensory world that helps autistic children and children with disabilities explore feelings, routines, communication and daily-life skills — at their own pace.

🧸 ☁️ 🎨 🫧 🌈 🎵

What is HARSHIVOS?

It isn't a menu of buttons. It's a place. Children enter a gentle world at dusk and discover everything by touching living objects — a cloud to name a feeling, a storybook to learn, a bubble machine to breathe, a cozy home for daily routines. The therapy is invisible; the play is everything.

  • Calm, predictable, sensory-friendly. Designed with — and for — an autistic child.
  • Free for every family that needs it.
